# Content Creator v1.0 (Frozen)

## Overview

Content Creator v1.0 is now in maintenance mode.

The module is considered stable, production-ready, and feature-complete for its current scope. The product team will preserve existing behavior and avoid structural redesign while active development focus moves to AI Director Studio.

## Completed Features

- Idea generation and exploration workflow
- Content creation workspace with guided editing
- Saved items and saved project flows
- Delete with confirmation behavior for saved entries
- Local persistence for creator history and studio workspaces
- Incremental autosave status feedback in production workspace surfaces
- Production workspace handoff for short-video style projects
- Existing dashboard, storyboard, and production progress surfaces in AI Director Studio

## Known Limitations

Future enhancements are intentionally deferred while this module is frozen:

- Advanced multi-format export templates
- Rich collaborative editing and roles
- Real-time co-authoring and comments
- Cross-project analytics and deep reporting
- End-to-end publishing automations

## Development Policy

- Content Creator remains stable and frozen at v1.0.
- Only incremental fixes, maintenance, and reliability improvements are allowed.
- No major workflow redesigns or broad feature expansion in Content Creator.
- AI Director Studio is the active feature focus moving forward.
- New UX and production pipeline innovation should land in AI Director Studio first.
